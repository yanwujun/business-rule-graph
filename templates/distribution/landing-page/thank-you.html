What happens next

Three steps from "payment confirmed" to "report in your inbox."

  1. 1 Within 24 hours. You'll receive an email from hello@roam-code.com with the one-page SOW for countersignature, a read-only deploy-key invitation to the temporary working tree we'll use, and a link to schedule the walk-through call.
  2. 2 Kickoff (day after countersignature). Once the SOW is countersigned and the deploy key is approved, the replay starts. Team report ships within 5 business days. Deep report ships within 10 business days from kickoff.
  3. 3 Walk-through call + report delivery. Founder walks the report with your team for 30 minutes (Team) or 90 minutes (Deep), answers questions, and discusses the recommended CI gates. The polished Markdown + PDF lands right after the call.

After delivery: temporary clone is deleted, the engagement record is yours on request, and the optional 50% credit toward Roam Review activates if you subscribe within 60 days.
